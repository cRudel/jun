Helen Morgan School

This school was my elementary school, also known as primary school. I was here from first to fifth grade, with a single teacher for the whole year for one grade. My favorite class in Helen Morgan was third grade with Mrs. Ruvo.

Memory from this school

In third grade towards the end of the year the class was introduced to musical instruments to see if we were interested in playing. I wanted to play a stringed instrument, but I didn't want to be just like my sister who played the cello so I picked the viola.
